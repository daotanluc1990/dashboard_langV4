# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Owner/CEO and operators of Com Tam Lang use this dashboard during daily business review, store follow-up, and performance checks. They need to scan revenue, portions, margin, store health, channel mix, inventory, people, customer feedback, and expansion readiness without digging through the source Google Sheet.

## Product Purpose

The dashboard turns Google Sheet operating data into a fast executive control room. Success means the user can answer: what changed, where the issue is, which branch/channel/shift matters, and what to do next. Filtering by day, month, year, custom ranges, channel, branch, shift, and comparison period must feel dependable.

## Brand Personality

Fresh, decisive, operational. The product should feel like a modern F&B command dashboard: lively enough for a restaurant brand, but calm and precise enough for financial and operational decisions.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dark SaaS dashboards, heavy navy fintech styling, decorative gradients, glassmorphism, oversized marketing-style hero sections, cramped spreadsheet clones, and chart palettes that look random or purely decorative.

## Design Principles

- Data first: every layout choice should make numbers, comparisons, and exceptions easier to read.
- Fresh operations: use color to separate business meaning, not to decorate inactive surfaces.
- Fast scanning: KPI, filter, chart, and table hierarchy must support quick daily review.
- Trust through consistency: controls, tables, cards, states, and responsive behavior should use one shared vocabulary.
- Mobile is review mode: on small screens, prioritize readable cards, short navigation, clear filters, and no floating controls covering content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Do not rely on color alone for status. Respect reduced motion. Keep labels readable on mobile and avoid fixed overlays blocking inputs or table content.
